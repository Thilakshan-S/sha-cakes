About Sha Cake

4.7 Customer Rating

Hatton's Favorite Cake Shop

Welcome to Sha Cake, your trusted neighborhood bakery nestled in the heart of Hatton, Sri Lanka. Located at the DKW Shopping Complex along the scenic Hatton–Maskeliya–Dalhousie Road, we've been crafting exquisite cakes that bring joy to every celebration.

From towering wedding cakes to whimsical birthday creations, every confection that leaves our kitchen is a masterpiece crafted with premium ingredients, artistic flair, and a whole lot of love.
